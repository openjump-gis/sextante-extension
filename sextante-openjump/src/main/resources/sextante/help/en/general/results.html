Results

Sextante extensions can also generate dialog boxes with different information. In most cases, tose information are of three types: text, images and tables.

This information is managed in SEXTANTE through the window called Results, where text, graphics and tables (that have been generated in a session are saved).

The results are stored temporarily, but can be saved as .html files, in the case of text output, .png files for the case of graphics, .cvs files for tables.

To do this, click the right button mouse and select the option "Save".
